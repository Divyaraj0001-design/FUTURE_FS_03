Meet Our Chef

The Art of Culinary Mastery

Chef Vikram Malhotra

With over 20 years of experience in prestigious kitchens across India and internationally, Chef Vikram brings a wealth of knowledge and passion to every dish. His philosophy is simple: respect the ingredients, honor tradition, and never stop innovating.

Trained in the finest culinary institutes of India and having worked in Michelin-starred restaurants worldwide, Chef Vikram has mastered the art of blending traditional Indian flavors with contemporary techniques. His dedication to excellence and creative approach to cuisine have earned him numerous accolades and a loyal following.

At Savoria, Chef Vikram leads a talented team of culinary artists, each bringing their unique skills and perspectives to create unforgettable dining experiences. Together, they craft menus that celebrate fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and showcase the rich diversity of Indian cuisine with a modern twist.

- Chef Vikram Malhotra
